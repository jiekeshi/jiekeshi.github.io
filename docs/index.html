I am Jieke, a third-year Ph.D. candidate and a Research Engineer at the School of Computing and Information Systems (SCIS), Singapore Management University (SMU). I am working with Professor David Lo as my supervisor.

My research interest primarily lies in the intersection of Software Engineering (SE) and Artificial Intelligence (AI). Particularly, I focus on (1) quality assurance of AI-enabled systems from an SE perspective, and (2) efficiency improvement of code models for real-world deployment. My work has been published in high-quality SE venues such as ICSE, ASE, TSE, etc. I have won/been nominated for several research paper awards.

Before coming to SMU, I received a B.S. degree from Yangzhou University (advised by Professor Junwu Zhu). I also worked as a research assistant at the Institute of Computing Technology, China Academy of Sciences (advised by Dr. Shi Wang).
